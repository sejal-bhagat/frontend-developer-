How to Control Body Odor Naturally

Body odor can be controlled with proper hygiene, lemon juice, baking soda, and apple cider vinegar. Simple lifestyle changes and home remedies can help keep you fresh throughout the day.

  • Maintain Proper Hygiene: Bathe daily and clean sweat-prone areas like underarms and feet properly.
  • Use Lemon Juice: Lemon has antibacterial properties that help reduce odor-causing bacteria.
  • Apply Baking Soda: Baking soda absorbs sweat and neutralizes unpleasant smells.
  • Apple Cider Vinegar: Helps balance skin pH and reduce bacterial growth.
  • Wear Breathable Fabrics: Choose cotton clothes to reduce sweating.
  • Stay Hydrated: Drinking enough water helps flush out toxins from the body.
  • Eat a Balanced Diet: Avoid excessive spicy foods, garlic, and processed foods that may increase body odor.

If body odor persists despite proper care, consult a healthcare professional to rule out underlying medical conditions.
