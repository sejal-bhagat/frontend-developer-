Editorial Policy

We aim to provide content that is credible, authoritative and crisp. Our goal atTeztecchCare is to make quality and affordable healthcare accessible to one and all. Furthermore, we aim to raise awareness about topics related to health and various diseases.

Medically correct information can be a tool for disease prevention and help readers make healthy choices. In order to get there, we maintain very strict standards to ensure you get well-researched and comprehensive healthcare information.

Our team of doctors, medical experts and editors curates and reviews all of our evidence-based content.

We follow a 6-stage review & editorial process for all our content creation.

1

Research & Topic Selection

We begin by selecting appropriate and relevant topics. Our team conducts thorough research to collate a list of credible sources including WHO, CDC, ICMR etc.

2

Structure Of The Content

After the idea is validated, it is slotted into a content category. Every content piece follows a well-defined structure for consistency.

3

Content Creation

When developing content, our team consults various reliable medical and scientific sources, such as trusted medical journals, review articles, textbooks, regulatory websites, and international and national treatment guidelines.

4

References

All facts, claims, and statistics presented in our content are supported by the latest and most relevant medical and scientific references.

5

Content Review

After content creation, our editors review it for language consistency, grammar, and readability to ensure high quality standards.

6

Feedback And Regular Updates

The team regularly considers user feedback and blog comments to improve the quality of our content and keep it updated.

Framework for Content Creation

We have set parameters that we follow strictly while generating healthcare and medical content. This ensures our information is rich and highly credible. The parameters are:

✔

Fact and medical science based

Publish content that is backed by medical evidence.

✔

Credible

Add references and citations from where information is sourced.

✔

Meets user needs

Ensure content is in line with user requirements and in reader-friendly language.

✔

Adhere to copyright laws

Ensure all content is original or properly attributed.

✔

Refrain from self-diagnosis & prevent misinformation

Ensure content does not spread false information.

✔

In sync with the latest developments

Ensure content is regularly updated with the latest medical research.

✔

Unbiased

Generate neutral content that isn’t biased towards a product or service.

✔

Adhere to legal standards

Strictly adhere to regulatory guidelines.

✔

Avoid treatment claims

Do not make claims about curing any disease or condition.

✔

Handle sensitive topics carefully

Avoid misleading statements, especially concerning serious illnesses.
