Benefits of Pumpkin Seeds

Pumpkin seeds are rich in protein, magnesium, antioxidants, iron, and healthy fats. They support heart health, improve immunity, and promote overall well-being.

  • Supports Heart Health: Magnesium and healthy fats help maintain normal blood pressure and improve cardiovascular function.
  • Boosts Immunity: High levels of antioxidants and zinc strengthen the immune system.
  • Improves Sleep Quality: Pumpkin seeds contain tryptophan, which may help promote better sleep.
  • Supports Prostate and Bladder Health: They are known to help in maintaining urinary health.
  • Rich in Protein: A great plant-based protein source for vegetarians.
  • Good for Skin & Hair: Antioxidants and vitamins help maintain healthy skin and strong hair.

Adding a small handful of pumpkin seeds to your daily diet can provide multiple health benefits.
