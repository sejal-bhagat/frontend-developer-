Simple Home Remedies for Swollen Gums

DOCTOR'S SPEAK / HOME REMEDIES / ORAL HEALTH
By Dr Smita Barode

Swollen gums are a common oral health issue caused by plaque buildup, infections, vitamin deficiencies, or hormonal changes. If left untreated, they may lead to bleeding gums and periodontal disease.

Effective Home Remedies

  • Salt Water Rinse: Gargle with warm salt water twice daily to reduce inflammation.
  • Turmeric Paste: Apply turmeric paste gently on gums.
  • Cold Compress: Use a cold compress on the cheek for pain relief.
  • Aloe Vera Gel: Massage fresh aloe vera gel on gums.
  • Maintain Oral Hygiene: Brush and floss regularly.

When to Consult a Dentist

If swelling lasts more than a week or is accompanied by pain, bleeding, or pus discharge, consult a dentist immediately.

Note: These home remedies provide temporary relief and are not a substitute for professional dental care.
