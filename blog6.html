Simple Home Remedies for Frequent Urination

DOCTOR'S SPEAK / HEALTH TODAY
By Dr Rajeev Singh

Frequent urination may occur due to infections or lifestyle habits. Simple home remedies can help manage symptoms.

🔎Common Causes:

  • Urinary Tract Infection (UTI)
  • Drinking too much water, tea, or coffee
  • Diabetes
  • Pregnancy
  • Anxiety
  • Overactive bladder

🏠 Simple Home Remedies:

  • Drink adequate (not excessive) water
  • Avoid caffeine and alcohol
  • Practice bladder training
  • Do pelvic floor (Kegel) exercises
  • Maintain proper hygiene
  • Consume cranberry juice (for mild UTI prevention)
